# Ad Network Comparison for AI Pulse

## Best Options for Tech/AI Websites

### 1. Google AdSense
**Best for:** Beginners, general audience
- **Pros:** Easy setup, largest advertiser network, automatic optimization
- **Cons:** Lower RPM for tech sites ($1-3), strict policies
- **Requirements:** Original content, 6+ months old (flexible), no traffic minimum
- **Apply:** https://www.google.com/adsense

### 2. Carbon Ads ⭐ (Recommended)
**Best for:** Developer/designer audience
- **Pros:** Tech-focused advertisers, non-intrusive, premium rates ($3-8 RPM)
- **Cons:** Invite-only or application required
- **Requirements:** Tech/design content, decent traffic
- **Apply:** https://www.carbonads.net

### 3. EthicalAds
**Best for:** Privacy-conscious tech sites
- **Pros:** No tracking, developer-focused, supports open source
- **Cons:** Smaller network than AdSense
- **Requirements:** Technical content
- **Apply:** https://www.ethicalads.io

### 4. BuySellAds
**Best for:** Direct ad sales
- **Pros:** Set your own prices, direct relationships
- **Cons:** Need significant traffic (50K+ monthly)
- **Revenue:** $5-20+ RPM depending on niche
- **Apply:** https://www.buysellads.com

### 5. Mediavine
**Best for:** High-traffic sites
- **Pros:** Premium rates ($10-30 RPM), excellent support
- **Cons:** 50,000 monthly sessions minimum
- **Apply:** https://www.mediavine.com

### 6. Ezoic
**Best for:** AI-driven optimization
- **Pros:** Machine learning optimization, any traffic level (now)
- **Cons:** Can slow down site
- **Apply:** https://www.ezoic.com

### 7. Affiliate Marketing (Additional Revenue)
**Best for:** Tech product recommendations
- Amazon Associates (1-10% commission)
- AI tool affiliate programs (OpenAI, Claude, etc.)
- Software referrals (NVIDIA, cloud providers)

## My Recommendation for AI Pulse

**Phase 1 (Starting out):**
1. Apply to Google AdSense (easiest approval)
2. Add affiliate links to AI tools mentioned

**Phase 2 (Growing):**
1. Apply to Carbon Ads (better fit for tech audience)
2. Join BuySellAds for direct sponsorships

**Phase 3 (Established):**
1. Move to Mediavine (highest RPM)
2. Negotiate direct deals with AI companies
