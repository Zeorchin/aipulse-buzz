⚔️ Team Building Guide

Recommended team compositions for different playstyles and content.

Team Type Core Members Strategy
Physical DPS Rossi, Chen Qianyu, Wulfgard, Ardelia Focus on Vulnerability stacks and Breach for massive physical damage.
Heat/Fire Laevatain, Avywenna, Xaihi, Antal Build Heat inflictions and trigger Combustion reactions.
Balanced F2P Endministrator, Perlica, Chen Qianyu, Ember Well-rounded team using mostly free characters.

🔥 Element System

Understanding elemental reactions is key to building effective teams.

Element Infliction Effect
Physical Vulnerability Increases physical damage taken
Fire Heat Infliction Deals continuous fire damage
Ice Solidification Freezes enemies, stops movement
Electric Electrification Chains lightning to nearby enemies
