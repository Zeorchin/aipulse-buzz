Cut Guide

Learn how to use the Cut move in Pokemon Pokopia

Home › Moves › Cut

Cut is a move that allows you to cut down trees and tall grass in Pokemon Pokopia. Check out the effects of Cut, how to learn Cut, and how to use it.

Cut Effects

Cut Down Trees

Cut allows you to chop down trees to clear space for building or to obtain Lumber material. This is essential for expanding your island and creating new habitats.

Cut Tall Grass

Cut can also be used to cut down tall grass. When you cut grass, you can obtain Leaf material. This is useful for crafting and completing requests.

How to Learn Cut

Find a Pokemon with Cut

Cut can be learned from various Pokemon throughout the game. Look for Pokemon that can teach you this move in the Withered Wasteland area.

Complete Their Request

Once you find a Pokemon that knows Cut, complete their request to learn the move.

How to Use Cut

Press ZR to Use Cut

Make sure you have Cut as your active move, which should be visible on the bottom right section of your screen. With Cut as your active move, a square in front of you will be highlighted.

Press ZR on your controller to cut down the highlighted tree or grass.
