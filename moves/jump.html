Jump Guide

Learn how to use the Jump move in Pokemon Pokopia

Home › Moves › Jump

Jump is a move that allows you to jump over obstacles in Pokemon Pokopia. Check out the effects of Jump, how to learn it, and how to use it.

Jump Effects

Jump Over Obstacles

Jump allows you to hop over small obstacles like fences, small rocks, and other barriers. This is useful for shortcuts and accessing new areas.

Mini Game: Jump Rope

Jump is also used in the Jump Rope mini game, where you can earn money and prizes by jumping rope consecutively.

How to Learn Jump

Find a Pokemon with Jump

Jump can be learned from many Pokemon early in the game. Check with various Pokemon to learn this basic move.

How to Use Jump

Press ZR to Jump

Make sure you have Jump as your active move. Press ZR to jump over obstacles in front of you.
