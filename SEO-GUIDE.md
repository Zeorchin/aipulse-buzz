# SEO Guide for AI Pulse

## What I've Added to Your Site

### 1. ✅ robots.txt
Located at: `https://aipulse.buzz/robots.txt`

This tells search engine crawlers which pages they can access. It also points to your sitemap.

### 2. ✅ Sitemap.xml
Located at: `https://aipulse.buzz/sitemap.xml`

This is a map of all your website pages that helps search engines find and index your content. It includes:
- All main pages (Home, News, Models, Benchmarks, etc.)
- All articles
- Gaming pages
- Priority levels and update frequencies

### 3. ✅ Meta Tags Added to Pages
Each page now has:
- **Title tag** - Appears in search results
- **Meta description** - Summary shown in search results
- **Keywords** - Helps search engines understand content
- **Canonical URL** - Prevents duplicate content issues
- **Open Graph tags** - For Facebook/LinkedIn sharing
- **Twitter Card tags** - For Twitter sharing
- **Structured data (JSON-LD)** - Helps search engines understand your content

### 4. ✅ Favicon
Added a simple emoji favicon that appears in browser tabs

## What YOU Need to Do Next

### Step 1: Submit to Google Search Console
1. Go to: https://search.google.com/search-console
2. Sign in with your Google account
3. Click "Add Property" → "URL prefix"
4. Enter: `https://aipulse.buzz/`
5. Verify ownership (choose "HTML tag" method)
6. Add the verification meta tag to your index.html `<head>` section
7. Submit your sitemap:
   - Go to "Sitemaps" in the left menu
   - Enter: `sitemap.xml`
   - Click "Submit"

### Step 2: Submit to Bing Webmaster Tools
1. Go to: https://www.bing.com/webmasters
2. Sign in with Microsoft account
3. Add your site: `https://aipulse.buzz/`
4. Verify ownership
5. Submit your sitemap

### Step 3: Create Social Share Images (Optional but Recommended)
Create images for better social sharing:
- `https://aipulse.buzz/images/og-image.jpg` (1200x630px) - Main site
- `https://aipulse.buzz/images/og-news.jpg` (1200x630px) - News page
- `https://aipulse.buzz/images/og-articles.jpg` (1200x630px) - Articles page

These appear when people share your site on Facebook, Twitter, LinkedIn.

### Step 4: Indexing Request (Immediate)
After submitting to Google Search Console:
1. Go to "URL Inspection" tool
2. Enter your homepage URL
3. Click "Request Indexing"
4. Do this for your main pages (News, Articles, etc.)
