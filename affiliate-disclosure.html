Transparency about how we monetize AI Pulse

Our Commitment to Transparency

AI Pulse is an independent publication dedicated to providing accurate, unbiased information about artificial intelligence. To support our work, we participate in various affiliate marketing programs and display advertisements.

What Are Affiliate Links?

Affiliate links are special URLs that track referrals from our website to AI service providers. When you click these links and subsequently make a purchase or sign up for a service, we may receive a commission at no additional cost to you.

How It Works

On our AI Models page, you'll see "Try [Model]" buttons. These are affiliate links that take you directly to the AI service provider's website. If you sign up or make a purchase after clicking, we earn a commission.

Our Editorial Independence

Importantly:

  • We recommend AI tools based on merit, not commission rates
  • Our benchmark data and analysis remain independent
  • We disclose affiliate relationships prominently
