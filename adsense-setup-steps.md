## After AdSense Approves You (1-2 days)

### 4. Create Ad Units
In AdSense dashboard:
1. Go to **Ads > Overview**
2. Click **"Create Ad Unit"**
3. Choose formats:
   - **Display ads** (banner)
   - **In-article ads** (within content)
   - **Multiplex ads** (grid of ads)
