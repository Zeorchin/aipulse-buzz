Overview

Pokemon TCG Pocket brings the classic Pokemon Trading Card Game to mobile devices with a streamlined 20-card deck format. Open digital booster packs, build decks, and battle players worldwide.

Key Features

  • 20-Card Decks: Faster games, no Energy cards needed
  • Immersive Cards: Beautiful card art with 3D effects
  • Trading: Trade cards with friends (coming soon)
  • Free Packs: Open 2 free booster packs every 12 hours
