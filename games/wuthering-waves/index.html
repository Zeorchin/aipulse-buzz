Echoes List

All echoes with sonata effects and how to use them

4-Cost Echoes (Main Echo)

Bell-Borne Geochelone

Rejuvenating Glow / Moonlit Clouds

Activate protection, dealing Glacio DMG. Reduce incoming damage, increase damage dealt.

Crownless

Havoc Eclipse

Transform into Crownless and assault enemies, dealing Havoc DMG. Increase Havoc DMG and Resonance Skill DMG.

Dragon of Dirge

Tidebreaking Courage

Transform into Dragon of Dirge and summon a Grief Rift, dealing Fusion DMG over time.

Dreamless

Havoc Eclipse

Transform into Dreamless to continuously attack enemies and deal Havoc DMG.

Feilian Beringal

Sierra Gale

Transform into Feilian Beringal to attack enemies, dealing Aero DMG.

Hecate

Empyrean Anthem

Summon Crescent Servants to deal Havoc DMG. Increases Coordinated Attack DMG.

Impermanence Heron

Moonlit Clouds

Transform into Impermanence Heron to deal Havoc DMG. Restore Resonance Energy on hit.

Inferno Rider

Molten Rift

Transform into Inferno Rider to perform consecutive strikes, dealing Fusion DMG.

Jué

Celestial Light

Summon Jué to attack enemies, dealing Spectro DMG. Sustained Spectro DMG over time.

Lampylumen Myriad

Freezing Frost

Transform into Lampylumen Myriad to continuously attack enemies, dealing Glacio DMG.

Liberty of Glory

Flaming Clawprint

Summon the Halberd of Glory to deal Fusion DMG. Gain Fusion and Resonance Liberation DMG Bonus.

Mech Abomination

Lingering Tunes

Deal Electro DMG to enemies and create Mech Waste that explodes. Increase ATK.

Mourning Aix

Celestial Light

Transform into Mourning Aix to continuously attack enemies, dealing Spectro DMG.

Tempest Mephis

Void Thunder

Transform into Tempest Mephis to continuously attack enemies, dealing Electro DMG.

Thundering Mephis

Void Thunder

Transform into Thundering Mephis to deal Electro DMG. Increase Electro DMG and Resonance Liberation DMG.

3-Cost Echoes (Sub Echo)

Stonewall Bracer

Rejuvenating Glow / Moonlit Clouds

Create a shield and deal Glacio DMG. Good for survivability and healing.

Flautist

Void Thunder / Lingering Tunes

Deal Electro DMG and recover Concerto Energy on hit.

Chasm Guardian

Rejuvenating Glow / Lingering Tunes

Perform a powerful strike dealing Havoc DMG. Lose HP then recover over time.

Glacio Dreadmane

Freezing Frost / Moonlit Clouds

Transform into Glacio Dreadmane to deal consecutive attacks. Can be cast mid-air.

Havoc Dreadmane

Molten Rift / Havoc Eclipse

Transform into Havoc Dreadmane to launch tail strikes, dealing Havoc DMG.

Cyan-Feathered Heron

Sierra Gale / Celestial Light

Charge at enemies dealing Aero DMG and interrupt enemy Special Skills.

Lightcrusher

Celestial Light

Lunge forward dealing Spectro DMG. Hold for longer distance after gathering momentum.
