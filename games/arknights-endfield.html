Overview

Arknights: Endfield is a real-time 3D strategy RPG set in the Arknights universe. Players explore the planet Talos-II as the Endministrator, building factories, managing resources, and engaging in tactical combat against the Blight.

Key Features

  • AIC Factory System: Design and automate production lines
  • Squad Combat: Control 4-member teams in real-time
  • Base Building: Construct and expand your industrial complex
  • Exploration: Discover the mysteries of Talos-II

Factory Building Guide

AIC System Basics

The Automated Industrial Complex (AIC) is the core of Endfield's gameplay. Build production chains to gather resources automatically.

Starter Tips:

  • Plan your factory layout before building
  • Use conveyor belts efficiently
  • Balance power generation with consumption
  • Create dedicated zones for different resources
