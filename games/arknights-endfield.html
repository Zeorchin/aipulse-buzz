📖 Game Overview

Last Updated: April 7, 2026

What is Arknights: Endfield?

Arknights: Endfield is a real-time 3D strategy RPG with factory-building elements set in the Arknights universe. Play as the Endministrator exploring planet Talos-II, building automated factories, and leading squads in tactical combat against the Blight.

Key Features

  • AIC Factory System: Design complex automated production lines
  • Real-time Combat: Control 4-member squads with unique abilities
  • Base Building: Construct and expand your industrial empire
  • Exploration: Uncover the mysteries of Talos-II
  • Gacha System: Collect 40+ unique operators

🚀 Quick Start for Beginners

  1. Complete the Tutorial - Learn basic combat and factory mechanics
  2. Claim Pre-registration Rewards - Get free 5-star operator Snowshine
  3. Build Basic Production - Set up iron and copper processing
  4. Level Your Team - Focus on 1 DPS, 1 Tank, 1 Healer
  5. Join the Community - Check Discord for meta strategies

🏭 Factory Building Guide

AIC System Basics

The Automated Industrial Complex (AIC) is the core of Endfield's gameplay. Design efficient production lines to automate resource gathering and processing.

📐 Planning Layout

  • Leave space for expansion
  • Group similar production together
  • Use straight conveyor lines when possible
  • Plan power distribution early

⚡ Power Management

  • Always have 20% extra power capacity
  • Use accumulators for backup
  • Upgrade to better power sources ASAP
  • Balance power across districts

🔄 Efficiency Tips

  • Use splitters to balance belts
  • Underground belts save space
  • Buffer critical resources
  • Use logistics drones late-game

⚔️ Combat System Guide

Squad Formation

Build a balanced 4-member squad with these roles:

Tank Front line, takes damage
Main DPS Primary damage dealer
Support Heals & buffs
Flex Second DPS or utility

Combat Tips

  • Dodge Red Zones: Enemy attacks show warning areas
  • Skill Rotation: Don't spam skills - time them for max effect
  • Switch Characters: Use tactical pause to swap squad members
  • Elemental Matchups: Some enemies are weak to specific elements
  • Ultimate Combo: Chain ultimates for massive damage

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Is Arknights: Endfield free to play?

Yes, the game is free-to-play with gacha mechanics for obtaining new characters. There are no paywalls for story content.

What's the difference between Arknights and Endfield?

Arknights is a 2D tower defense gacha game. Endfield is a 3D real-time strategy RPG with factory building elements set in the same universe.

How do I get more characters?

Characters are obtained through the gacha system using T-Creds or Headhunt Permits. You also get Snowshine (5★) for free from pre-registration.

What platform is best to play on?

PC offers the best experience for factory building with mouse and keyboard. Mobile is great for quick daily tasks. PS5 has enhanced graphics.

Is there multiplayer?

Currently Endfield is single-player only. Co-op modes are planned for future updates according to the developer roadmap.
