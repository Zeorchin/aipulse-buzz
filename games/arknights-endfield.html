📘 Beginner's Guide

🎯 First 24 Hours - Priority Checklist

🆕 Getting Started

  1. Choose Your Starter

    You'll get Snowshine (5★ Specialist) free. She's excellent for beginners - use her!

  2. Understand the Combat

    It's real-time action, not turn-based. Learn to dodge (red zones) and switch characters.

  3. Factory Basics

    Start with miners → smelters → storage. Keep it simple early on.

  4. Resource Priority

    Focus on: Iron > Copper > Coal > Power. Everything else comes later.

⚠️ Common Mistakes

❌
Spreading Resources Too Thin

Don't level every character. Focus on your main 4.

❌
Neglecting Factory Power

Always have 20% extra power capacity before expanding.

❌
Spending T-Creds on Standard Banner

Only pull on rate-up banners with good characters.

❌
Ignoring Equipment

Upgrade gear! It makes a huge difference in combat.

🔄 Reroll Guide

Should You Reroll?

⚠️ We Do NOT Recommend Rerolling

While rerolling can get you strong characters, the process is extremely tedious for these reasons:

  • No guest account system - Need new email for each attempt
  • 20-45 minutes per roll - Must play until Valley Reboot quest
  • 0.8% rate for 6★ - No pity on first pull session
  • Free 6★ from events - You'll get strong characters anyway

👹 Boss Strategy Guides

🐉

Typhon

Chapter 3 Final Boss
HARD
Element ❄️ Ice
Weakness 🔥 Fire
Recommended Level 35+

Key Mechanics

  • Frost Breath: Cone AoE that applies slow. Dodge to the sides.
  • Ice Spikes: Red circles appear on ground. Move immediately.
  • Enrage at 30% HP: Attack speed increases by 50%. Save ultimate for this phase.

Strategy

  1. Bring Surtur or any fire DPS for elemental advantage
  2. Keep Legion (or any tank) in front to absorb damage
  3. When boss flies up, prepare to dodge - it will dive bomb
  4. At 30% HP, use all ultimates for burst damage

First Clear Rewards

💎 T-Creds ×500 📘 Epic Combat Records ×5 ⚔️ Frost Resistance Chip
🤖

AIC-Prototype 07

Chapter 2 Boss
MEDIUM

Key Mechanics

  • Drone Swarm: Summons helper drones - kill them fast
  • Laser Beam: Straight line attack - dodge perpendicular
  • Self-Repair: Boss heals if you don't interrupt - use crowd control

Strategy

This boss is all about target priority. Kill drones immediately or they'll overwhelm you. Any team composition works here.

🏛️ Outpost & Liaison System

What are Outposts?

Outposts are satellite settlements that expand your factory capabilities and provide Stock Bills through trading. Each outpost can be upgraded and assigned an Operator Liaison for bonus effects.

Operator Liaison Guide

Assign operators to outposts based on their Faction, Hobbies, and Expertise. Matching traits activate Outpost Skills:

Skill 1 - Faction

20% chance to earn +1 Outpost Prosperity per point earned via trading

Skill 2 - Hobbies

20% chance to earn +1 Stock Bill per bill earned via trading

Skill 3 - Expertise

20% chance to earn +1 Stock Bill per bill earned by outpost regeneration

Priority Guide:

  1. Skill 3 first - Overall increases Stock Bill regeneration
  2. Skill 1 before max level - Helps level outpost faster
  3. Skill 2 after max level - Better when no more Prosperity needed

Regional Development Management (RDM)

Increase your Regional Development Level by:

  • Rigging mining spots for the first time
  • Upgrading Outposts
  • Unlocking Recycling Stations
  • Completing exploration objectives

Benefits: Higher Mining Purity, additional resource nodes, better trade prices.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Is Arknights: Endfield free to play?

Yes! The game is completely free-to-play. You can experience all story content without spending money. The gacha system is for obtaining new characters faster, but you can earn currency through gameplay.

What's the difference between Arknights and Endfield?

Arknights is a 2D tower defense game. Endfield is a 3D real-time action RPG with factory building. They share the same universe but are completely different gameplay experiences.

Who should I pull for as a beginner?

Priority order: Laevatain (best DPS) > Fluorite (best healer) > Legion (best tank). If you can't get 6★ units, Snowshine (free 5★) is excellent!

How do I save my factory designs?

Open the blueprint menu (B key), select "Create Blueprint", drag to select your design, and give it a name. You can share codes with other players!

Is there multiplayer or co-op?

Currently single-player only. Co-op modes are planned for future updates according to the developer roadmap.

What platform is best?

PC is best for factory building with mouse/keyboard. Mobile is great for quick dailies. PS5 has the best graphics and performance.
