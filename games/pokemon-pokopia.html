Overview

Pokemon Pokopia is a life simulation game where players create and customize their own island paradise. Decorate your space, care for Pokemon, and visit friends' islands in this relaxing social experience.

Key Features

  • Island Customization: Design your perfect Pokemon paradise
  • Pokemon Friends: Various Pokemon visit and stay on your island
  • Social Visits: Explore other players' islands
  • Seasonal Events: Limited-time decorations and activities

Island Design Tips

Getting Started

  • Start with a theme (forest, beach, city, etc.)
  • Create pathways connecting different areas
  • Use elevation for visual interest
  • Leave space for future expansions
