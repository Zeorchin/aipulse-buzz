💡 Tips & Tricks

Expert advice to maximize your progress

⚔️ Combat Tips

1

Speed is Everything

Always prioritize speed on your DPS units. Going first means you can eliminate enemies before they act.

2

Elemental Advantage

Fire beats Ice, Ice beats Wind, Wind beats Earth, Earth beats Lightning, Lightning beats Fire. Check enemy elements before battle!

3

Team Composition

Always have at least one tank, one healer, and one DPS. The remaining spots can be support or secondary damage dealers.

💰 Economy Tips

1

Daily Energy Management

Use all your energy every day, but prioritize hard dungeons over easy stages. Efficiency matters more than farming easy content.

2

Save for Pity

Always save enough gems for 120 pulls to guarantee the rate-up character. It's better to skip a banner than to waste gems without pity.

3

Shop Prioritization

Buy awakening stones and skill books from the guild shop first. These are harder to obtain than regular materials.

📈 Progression Tips

1

Focus on 5 Heroes

Don't spread your resources too thin. Level up 5 heroes fully before investing in others. A maxed team beats a mediocre full roster.

2

Equipment Matters

Keep your gear upgraded. A well-equipped SR hero outperforms an ungeared SSR. Upgrade your best equipment regularly.

3

Join an Active Guild

Guild bonuses add up quickly. An active guild with daily donations can give you hundreds of free gems and materials weekly.

✅ Do's and Don'ts

✅ Do

  • Complete all daily quests
  • Save gems for limited banners
  • Upgrade your main team's skills
  • Join guild wars for rewards
  • Watch ads for free rewards
  • Save hero scrolls for events

❌ Don't

  • Waste gems on energy refills
  • Pull on every banner
  • Neglect skill upgrades
  • Ignore elemental advantages
  • Sell SSR equipment
  • Skip guild donations
