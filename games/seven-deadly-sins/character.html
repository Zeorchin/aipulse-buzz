Character Overview

Meliodas is the main protagonist and the captain of the Seven Deadly Sins. As the Dragon's Sin of Wrath, he possesses immense power and is one of the strongest characters in the game. His unique kit focuses on dealing massive fire damage while also providing utility through his defensive capabilities.

✅ Pros

  • Highest single-target damage in the game
  • Strong fire element synergy
  • Excellent survivability with heal
  • Great in both PvP and PvE content

❌ Cons

  • High investment required for optimal performance
  • Weak against ice enemies
  • Limited utility outside damage

Ultimate
Lv. 1
