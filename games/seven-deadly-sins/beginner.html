📖 Beginner's Guide

Everything new players need to know about The Seven Deadly Sins: Origin

🎮 Getting Started

Welcome to The Seven Deadly Sins: Origin! This guide will help you understand the core mechanics and progress efficiently through the game.

Your First Steps

  1. Complete the Main Story

    Progress through the main story quests to unlock new features, characters, and game modes. The early chapters are designed to teach you core mechanics.

  2. Build Your Team

    You'll receive several free characters early on. Focus on leveling up your main DPS and healer. Check our Tier List for character rankings.

  3. Upgrade Your Gear

    Equipment matters! Keep your weapons and armor updated. Visit Weapons and Armor pages for recommended builds.

  4. Join a Guild

    Once you reach Level 20, join a guild for bonus rewards, guild raids, and community support.

How to Get Resources

  • Gems: Daily login rewards, achievements, story progression, in-app purchases
  • Gold: Dungeons, selling items, guild donations, daily quests
  • Hero Scrolls: Events, login rewards, guild shop, milestone rewards
  • Energy: Regenerates 1 every 5 minutes, refills with gems
💡 Tip: Save your gems for banner pity pulls! The guaranteed SSR at 120 summons makes it worth waiting.

⚔️ Combat Basics

Understanding the combat system is crucial for success in both story content and PvP.

Character Roles

Role Description Best For
🗡️ DPS High damage dealers Farming, boss killing
🛡️ Tank High defense, protects team Difficult content, raids
✨ Healer Restores HP, provides buffs Extended battles
🏹 Support Debuffs enemies, buffs allies Team compositions

Elemental System

  • Fire 🔥 beats Ice ❄️
  • Ice ❄️ beats Wind 💨
  • Wind 💨 beats Earth 🪨
  • Earth 🪨 beats Lightning ⚡
  • Lightning ⚡ beats Fire 🔥
⚠️ Warning: Always check enemy elements before entering battle. Using the wrong element reduces damage by 50%!

📈 Progression Tips

Daily Checklist

  • Complete all daily quests
  • Use all daily energy
  • Collect login rewards
  • Check guild rewards
  • Claim arena rewards

Weekly Goals

  • Complete weekly dungeon challenges
  • Participate in guild raid
  • Clear tower floors
  • Stock up on upgrade materials
❌ Avoid These Mistakes:
  • Wasting gems on energy refills early game
  • Pulling on every banner without saving for pity
  • Ignoring gear upgrades
  • Not upgrading your hero's skills

🏆 Gacha & Summons

The banner system in Seven Deadly Sins: Origin follows a pity mechanism that guarantees results.

Pity System

Pull Count Guarantee
10 Pulls At least 1 SR or higher
50 Pulls At least 1 SSR
120 Pulls Rate-up character guaranteed

Best Banners to Pull

  • Character Banners: Focus on limited banners with rate-up SSRs
  • Weapon Banners: Good for completing your gear sets
  • Festival Banners: Usually have best rates and bonuses
