❓ Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get more gems? ▼
Gems can be obtained through: Daily login rewards, completing achievements, participating in events, ranking in arena, selling items, and in-app purchases. Save your gems for banner pity pulls!
What is the pity system? ▼
The pity system guarantees rewards after certain pull counts. After 10 pulls, you're guaranteed at least 1 SR+. After 50 pulls, you're guaranteed at least 1 SSR. After 120 pulls on a rate-up banner, you're guaranteed the rate-up character!
How do I upgrade my heroes? ▼
To upgrade heroes: Use EXP potions or run stages to level up. Use awakening stones to increase stats beyond max level. Use skill books to upgrade abilities. Use equipment to boost combat power.
Which characters should I build? ▼
Focus on SSR characters from tier lists, especially those with versatile abilities. Check our Tier List page for current rankings. Early game, any leveled hero works, but late game requires strategic team building.
How do I join a guild? ▼
Reach Level 20 to unlock the guild feature. Tap on "Guild" in the main menu, then browse available guilds or create your own. Join an active guild for maximum benefits!
What are the best weapons? ▼
SSR weapons are best, but SR weapons can work early game. Focus on weapons that match your main character's element and build. Check our Weapons page for detailed stats and recommendations.
How does elemental advantage work? ▼
Fire beats Ice, Ice beats Wind, Wind beats Earth, Earth beats Lightning, Lightning beats Fire. Using the right element deals 50% more damage. Using the wrong element deals 50% less damage!
Where can I find redeem codes? ▼
Follow official social media channels, join Discord server, subscribe to email newsletter, and check our Codes page for working redemption codes. Codes are released during events and celebrations!
