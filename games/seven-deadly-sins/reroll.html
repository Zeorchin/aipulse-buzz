Top Reroll Picks: Meliodas & Jericho

Meliodas is a high-DPS Darkness Attacker with strong Burst damage. As a Darkness Element unit, he maintains consistent output.

Jericho specializes in Dual Swords, providing efficient Burst Gauge generation and high sustained damage.

King and Elaine Are Highly Versatile

Both can flex into multiple roles: Attacker, Healer, and Support depending on weapon equipped.

Evaluation Criteria

Focus on early-game convenience, synergy with tutorial heroes, and boss performance.

Adventure Skills are highly valued - Drake, Mannie, Hendrickson, Dreyfus help with exploration.

Free characters (Tristan, Tioreh, Slater, Howzer) get lower priority since they're guaranteed.

Should You Reroll?

Mobile: Easiest - guest account allows quick rerolls. 57 pulls per attempt, ~15 min each.

PC/PS5: Requires multiple accounts - more time-consuming.

When to Stop Rerolling

Aim for Jericho or Meliodas - both fit any party composition.

You get 300+ free pulls by playing. Featured character guaranteed after 120 pulls, SSR after 80.
