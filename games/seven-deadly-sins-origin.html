Overview

The Seven Deadly Sins: Origin is an open-world action RPG based on the popular anime and manga series. Experience an original story while meeting beloved characters from the series in a vast, explorable world.

Key Features

  • Open World: Explore Britannia and beyond
  • Original Story: New narrative alongside the anime
  • Character Switching: Play as multiple Sins members
  • Co-op Mode: Team up with friends

Playable Characters

Meliodas

Dragon's Sin of Wrath

Balanced fighter with counter abilities and Hellblaze flames.

Ban

Fox's Sin of Greed

Agile thief with lifesteal and immortality abilities.

Diane

Serpent's Sin of Envy

Giant warrior with earth manipulation and heavy attacks.

King

Grizzly's Sin of Sloth

Spirit spear wielder with healing and ranged attacks.

Gowther

Goat's Sin of Lust

Ranged magic user with mind manipulation abilities.

Merlin

Boar's Sin of Gluttony

Mage with infinite magic and utility spells.

Combat System

Basic Combat

  • Light Attacks: Fast combo starters
  • Heavy Attacks: Break enemy defenses
  • Dodging: Perfect dodge for counter opportunities
  • Ultimate Skills: Powerful character-specific abilities

Advanced Techniques

  • Switch characters mid-combo for extended damage
  • Use environmental objects as weapons
  • Time your blocks to trigger perfect parries
  • Build up Ultimate gauges efficiently

Equipment Guide

Sacred Treasures

Each Sin member has their signature weapon that unlocks their full potential:

  • Demon Sword: Meliodas - Increases counter damage
  • Courechouse: Ban - Enhances lifesteal
  • Gideon: Diane - Boosts earth magic
  • Chastiefol: King - Strengthens spirit attacks
