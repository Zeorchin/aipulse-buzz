Dark Habitat Guide

How to make and maintain dark habitats

Home › Habitat › Dark Habitat

Dark habitats are essential for attracting Ghost-type and Dark-type Pokemon to your island.

How to Make a Dark Habitat

  1. Build a habitat structure using dark blocks
  2. Reduce lighting in the area
  3. Place shadow-reducing decorations
  4. Ensure no bright lights nearby

Pokemon That Need Dark Habitats

  • Gastly
  • Haunter
  • Gengar
  • Misdreavus
  • Shuppet
  • Duskull

Tips

  • Use dim lamps instead of bright ones
  • Place habitats away from bright areas
  • Use dark-colored furniture

Habitat Conditions

  • Dark
  • Bright
  • Dry
  • Humid
  • Warm
  • Cool
