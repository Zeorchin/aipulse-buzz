Dry Habitat Guide

Creating dry conditions for Pokemon

Home › Habitat › Dry

Dry habitats are essential for Ground-type and Rock-type Pokemon.

How to Create

  1. Use desert-themed decorations
  2. Avoid water sources nearby
  3. Use sand blocks
  4. Remove wet soil

Pokemon

  • Geodude
  • Graveler
  • Onix
  • Diglett
