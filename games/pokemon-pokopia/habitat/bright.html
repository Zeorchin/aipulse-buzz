Bright Habitat Guide

Creating bright habitats for Pokemon

Home › Habitat › Bright

Bright habitats are essential for Fire-type and Electric-type Pokemon.

How to Create

  1. Use bright lamps
  2. Add sunlight crystals
  3. Place in open areas
  4. Avoid dark decorations
