Crafting Guide

How to craft items in Pokemon Pokopia

Home › Crafting

Learn how to craft items using materials gathered from your island.

How to Craft

  1. Open your inventory
  2. Select the "Craft" option
  3. Choose an item to craft
  4. Confirm the materials needed
  5. Select "Craft" to create the item

Crafting Stations

  • Workbench - Basic crafting
  • Smelting Furnace - Metal items
  • 3D Printer - Special items

Tips

  • Gather materials daily for best results
  • Focus on essential items first
  • Use Pokemon with Gather specialty to help
