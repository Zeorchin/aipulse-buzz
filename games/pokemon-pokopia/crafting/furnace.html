Smelting Furnace Guide

How to use the smelting furnace

Home › Crafting › Smelting Furnace

The Smelting Furnace is used to create metal ingots from ore.

How to Use

  1. Place ore in the furnace
  2. Add fuel (wood or coal)
  3. Wait for the smelting process
  4. Collect your ingots

Items You Can Make

  • Copper Ingot - From Copper Ore
  • Iron Ingot - From Iron Ore
  • Gold Ingot - From Gold Ore
  • Pokemetal - From Pokemetal Fragments

Tips

  • Use coal for faster smelting
  • Stack multiple ores at once
  • Check the furnace regularly
