Professor Tangrowth

The main professor who guides you through the game

Home › NPCs › Professor Tangrowth

Professor Tangrowth is the main story NPC who guides you throughout your adventure in Pokopia.

Role

  • Provides the Pokedex
  • Gives starter Pokemon choice
  • Teaches about habitats
  • Unlocks new features

Location

Found at the Pokemon Center in Withered Wasteland.
