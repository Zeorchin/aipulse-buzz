About DJ Rotom

DJ Rotom is the resident music DJ in Pokemon Pokopia. Rotom possesses various electronics and can change the background music of your island.

Location

DJ Rotom can be found at the Rotom DJ Stand near the main plaza. He appears during evening hours and special events.

Services

  • Change Island Music - Select from various soundtracks
  • Special Events - Host music events
  • Rotom Phone - Customize phone ringtones
