About Mosslax

Mosslax is a sleepy Pokemon helper found in the Withered Wasteland area. He spends most of his time sleeping on soft moss patches, but when awake, he helps players find rare materials.

Location

Mosslax can be found sleeping near the mossy rocks in the Withered Wasteland. Visit him during daytime hours to receive materials.

Rewards

  • Mossy Rock - Wake him up with a whistle
  • Sleep Powder - Random material drop
  • Evolution items - Rare chance
