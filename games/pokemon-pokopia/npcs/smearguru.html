About Smearguru

Smearguru is the artistic NPC in Pokemon Pokopia who specializes in painting and photo mode. She can be found near the creative studio in Sparkling Skylands.

Location

Smearguru is located in the Sparkling Skylands, near the Photo Studio. Visit her to unlock new photo frames and painting styles.

Services

  • Photo Mode - Unlock new frames and filters
  • Painting - Create custom artwork of your Pokemon
  • Frame Customization - New frame designs
