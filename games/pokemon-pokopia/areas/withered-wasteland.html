Withered Wasteland

The starting area - A dried up desert landscape

Home › Areas › Withered Wasteland

Withered Wasteland is the first area you'll explore in Pokemon Pokopia.

Features

  • Desert terrain
  • Abandoned structures
  • Dried plants and vegetation
  • Pokemon Center location

Pokemon Found

  • Bulbasaur
  • Squirtle
  • Charmander
  • Caterpie
  • Pidgey

Tips

  • Use Water Gun to revive plants
  • Build Grass habitats first
  • Complete all requests to progress
