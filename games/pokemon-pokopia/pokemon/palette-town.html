About Palette Town Pokemon

Palette Town is a special location in Pokemon Pokopia where rare and unique Pokemon can be found. These Pokemon are not available in any other area.

How to Access

Palette Town is unlocked after completing the main story and reaching level 50. Speak to Professor Tangrowth to receive the Palette Key.
