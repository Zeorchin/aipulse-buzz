Getting Started

Welcome to Pokemon Pokopia! This comprehensive walkthrough will guide you through the entire main story, from the beginning to the end.

Tip: This guide assumes you're playing the game for the first time. If you're looking for specific tips, check out our Beginner's Guide.

Chapter 1: Arrival at Withered Wasteland

  1. Create your character and choose your starter Pokemon
  2. Meet Professor Tangrowth at the Pokemon Center
  3. Learn basic controls and how to explore
  4. Complete your first request
  5. Build your first habitat

Chapter 2: Expanding Your Island

  1. Invite your first wild Pokemon
  2. Learn how to craft items
  3. Complete requests for villagers
  4. Unlock new areas to explore

Chapter 3: Bleak Beach

  1. Travel to the beach area
  2. Meet new Pokemon suited for coastal habitats
  3. Unlock ocean-themed builds
  4. Complete the area's main request

Chapter 4: Rocky Ridges

  1. Discover the mountain region
  2. Find rare Pokemon in caves
  3. Learn advanced building techniques
  4. Defeat area challenges

Chapter 5: Sparkling Skylands

  1. Unlock the floating islands
  2. Face the final challenges
  3. Complete the main story
  4. Unlock postgame content
Missable Content: Some items and Pokemon can only be obtained during specific story chapters. Check our Missables Guide for details.
