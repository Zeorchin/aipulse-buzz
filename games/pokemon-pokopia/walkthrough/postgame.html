Postgame Guide

Everything to do after completing the main story

Home › Walkthrough › Postgame

Congratulations on completing the main story! Here's everything you can do in Pokemon Pokopia's postgame.

Complete Your Pokedex

Try to catch all available Pokemon to complete your Pokedex. Some Pokemon are only available in specific areas or during certain times.

Build Your Dream Island

  • Expand to all areas
  • Build every habitat type
  • Collect all furniture items
  • Create beautiful landscapes

Cloud Island Adventures

Visit other players' islands via Cloud Islands to:

  • Get rare items
  • Complete exclusive requests
  • Share your island

Events

Participate in limited-time events for exclusive rewards and Pokemon. Check the Events page for current activities.

Increase Trainer Rank

Complete more requests and expand your island to increase your Trainer Rank and unlock new features.
