Bleak Beach Guide

Coastal area with beach and ocean habitats

Home › Walkthrough › Bleak Beach

Bleak Beach is a coastal area in Pokemon Pokopia featuring sandy shores and ocean waters. This area unlocks after progressing through Withered Wasteland.

Overview

Bleak Beach offers a different environment from the desert landscape of Withered Wasteland. Here you'll find beaches, shallow waters, and opportunities for ocean-based activities.

Pokemon Found

  • Squirtle - Water type
  • Wartortle - Water type
  • Magikarp - Water type
  • Tentacool - Water/Poison type
  • Poliwag - Water type
  • Poliwhirl - Water type
  • Tentacruel - Water/Poison type

How to Unlock

Bleak Beach unlocks after completing the main story objectives in Withered Wasteland. Speak to Professor Tangrowth to access this new area.

Tips

  • Build Ocean habitats to attract Water-type Pokemon
  • Use Surf to explore deeper waters
  • Look for hidden items in the sand
