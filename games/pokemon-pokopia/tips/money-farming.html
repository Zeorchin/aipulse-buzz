Earning Pokopia Dollars

Money is essential in Pokemon Pokopia for buying items, expanding your island, and more. Here are the best ways to earn Pokopia Dollars!

Best Methods at a Glance:
  1. Sell items and resources
  2. Complete Pokemon requests
  3. Play mini games
  4. Visit Cloud Islands

Selling Items

The easiest way to make money is by selling items you don't need:

What to Sell

  • Berries - Sell excess berries for quick cash
  • Materials - Common materials fetch good prices
  • Furniture - Duplicate items can be sold
  • Special Items - Some items sell for high prices
Pro Tip: Check the-shop at Pokemon Centers to see current prices!

Complete Requests

Pokemon requests often reward you with Pokopia Dollars. Look for requests that offer:

  • High cash rewards
  • Items you can sell

Mini Games

Playing mini games is a fun way to earn extra money:

Jump Rope

The more consecutive jumps you do, the bigger the reward!

  • 100+ jumps: 1,000+ Pokopia Dollars
  • 200+ jumps: 2,000+ Pokopia Dollars
  • 300+ jumps: 5,000+ Pokopia Dollars

Hide and Seek

Find hidden Pokemon quickly for bonus rewards!

Cloud Island Visits

Visit other players' islands via Cloud Island to:

  • Collect items they leave
  • Complete exclusive requests
  • Get rare items to sell
Note: Some rare items from Cloud Islands can be sold for very high prices!

Daily Money Making Routine

  1. Check all Pokemon for items they may give you
  2. Complete any high-reward requests
  3. Play mini games (especially Jump Rope)
  4. Sell excess items
  5. Visit one Cloud Island

Money Saving Tips

While earning is important, saving is equally crucial:

  • Only buy what you need now
  • Wait for sales at shops
  • Craft items instead of buying when possible
  • Focus on versatile items over specialty ones
