Daily Activities

Complete list of things to do each day in Pokemon Pokopia

Home › Tips › Daily Activities

A new day starts at 5:00 AM in-game time. Here's everything you should do daily!

Morning Routine

  1. Check your Pokemon for items
  2. Collect berries from trees
  3. Water crops if you have any
  4. Check for new wild Pokemon

Main Activities

  • Complete Requests - Finish at least 3-5 requests daily
  • Visit Cloud Islands - Check other players' islands
  • Play Mini Games - Jump Rope and others for rewards
  • Gather Materials - Collect resources from your island

Evening Routine

  1. Save your game
  2. Check tomorrow's schedule
  3. Plan next day's activities
Tip: Use time travel to redo daily activities if needed!
