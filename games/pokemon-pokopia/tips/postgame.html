Postgame Guide

Everything to do after completing the main story

Home › Tips › Postgame Guide

New Areas Unlock

After completing the main story, several new areas and features become available:

  • Cloud Island - A floating island that appears in the sky
  • Hidden Valley - Secret area with rare Pokemon
  • Ancient Ruins - Archaeological site with artifacts

Endgame Pokemon

Many powerful Pokemon become available only after completing the main story:

  • Mega Evolution Pokemon
  • Primal Reversion Pokemon
  • Regional Variants
  • Special Event Pokemon

Postgame Quests

  • Complete all remaining Requests
  • Challenge the Battle Tower
  • Collect all Records
  • Master all Fishing Reels

Continuous Content

  • Daily events and activities
  • Weekly rotating Pokemon
  • Seasonal events
  • Community challenges
