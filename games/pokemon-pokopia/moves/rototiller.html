Rototiller Effects

Till Soil for Farming

Rototiller allows you to till soil and prepare it for planting crops. This is essential for farming in Pokopia.

Grow Crops Faster

Using Rototiller on soil can help crops grow faster and produce better yields.

How to Learn Rototiller

Find a Pokemon with Rototiller

Rototiller can be learned from Pokemon like Diglett, Dugtrio, or other ground-digging Pokemon.

How to Use Rototiller

Press ZR to Till

Make sure you have Rototiller as your active move. Press ZR on untilled soil to prepare it for planting.
