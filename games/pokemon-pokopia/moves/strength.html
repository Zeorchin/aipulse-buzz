Strength Guide

Learn how to use the Strength move in Pokemon Pokopia

Home › Moves › Strength

Strength is a move that allows you to push heavy objects in Pokemon Pokopia. Check out the effects of Strength, how to learn it, and how to use it.

Strength Effects

Push Heavy Objects

Strength allows you to push heavy boulders and objects that are too heavy to move normally. This is essential for solving puzzles and accessing new areas.

Clear Paths

Use Strength to push boulders out of the way and create new paths on your island.

How to Learn Strength

Find a Strong Pokemon

Strength can be learned from Fighting-type Pokemon or Pokemon with high attack stats. Look for Pokemon like Machop, Hitmonlee, or other fighting Pokemon.

How to Use Strength

Press ZR to Push

Make sure you have Strength as your active move. Press ZR near a heavy object to push it.
