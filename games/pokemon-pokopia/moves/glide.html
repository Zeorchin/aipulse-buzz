Glide Guide

Learn how to use the Glide transformation move in Pokemon Pokopia

Home › Moves › Glide

Glide is a transformation move that allows you to fly through the air in Pokemon Pokopia. Check out the effects of Glide, how to learn it, and how to use it.

Glide Effects

Fly Through the Air

Glide allows you to transform into a flying Pokemon and soar through the air. This is the fastest way to travel and allows you to reach high places.

Access Elevated Areas

Many areas and items in Pokopia are located at high elevations. Glide is essential for reaching places like the Sparkling Skylands.

How to Learn Glide

Find a Flying Pokemon

Glide can be learned from Flying-type Pokemon. Look for Pokemon like Pidgey, Wingull, or other flying Pokemon throughout the game.

How to Use Glide

Press ZR to Transform

Make sure you have Glide as your active move. Press ZR to transform into a flying Pokemon and take to the skies.

Use the control stick to steer while gliding.
