Suck Guide

Learn how to use the Suck move in Pokemon Pokopia

Home › Moves › Suck

Suck is a move that allows you to collect liquids and honey in Pokemon Pokopia. Check out the effects of Suck, how to learn it, and how to use it.

Suck Effects

Collect Liquids

Suck allows you to collect water and other liquids from various sources. This can be used to fill containers or collect water for your island.

Collect Honey

Suck can also be used to collect honey from flowers and honeycomb. This is useful for crafting and feeding certain Pokemon.

How to Learn Suck

Find a Pokemon with Suck

Suck can be learned from Pokemon like Bulbasaur, Oddish, or other Pokemon that can suck liquids.

How to Use Suck

Press ZR to Suck

Make sure you have Suck as your active move. Press ZR near a liquid source or flower to collect its contents.
