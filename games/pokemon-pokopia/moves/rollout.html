Rollout Guide

Learn how to use the Rollout move in Pokemon Pokopia

Home › Moves › Rollout

Rollout is a transformation move that allows you to roll across the ground in Pokemon Pokopia. Check out the effects of Rollout, how to learn it, and how to use it.

Rollout Effects

Roll Across Terrain

Rollout allows you to transform into a ball-like Pokemon and roll across the ground at high speed. This can be used for transportation and fun.

Break Obstacles

While rolling, you can break through weak walls and barriers.

How to Learn Rollout

Find a Pokemon with Rollout

Rollout can be learned from Pokemon like Diglett, Geodude, or other ball-shaped Pokemon.

How to Use Rollout

Press ZR to Roll

Make sure you have Rollout as your active move. Press ZR to transform and start rolling.
