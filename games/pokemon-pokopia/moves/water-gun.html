Water Gun Guide

Learn about the Water Gun move in Pokemon Pokopia

Home › Moves › Water Gun

Water Gun is a move that allows you to water and revive dry plants in Pokemon Pokopia. Check out the effects of Water Gun, how to use this move, and how to learn Water Gun.

List of Contents

  • Water Gun Effects

Water Gun Effects

Revives Dry Plants

Water Gun can be used to revive the dry plants around you. It can be used on dry grass, dried up flowers, withered trees, and even dry soil in order to bring life back to the greenery.

Revitalizing dried plants is required in order to make certain habitats and improve the surrounding area.

Water Dry Soil to Plant Grass

If you want to use Leafage to add grass to the ground, you need to water the area you want to plant it in. Otherwise, you will create dry grass instead of tall grass.

If you've already created dry grass, don't worry. It can still be watered in order to revive it into normal tall grass.

Water Crops to Grow

Seeds planted in tilled soil require water in order for the crops to grow. You can use Water Gun to water these crops if you do not have an irrigation system to automatically water them for you.

How to Learn Water Gun

Learn from Squirtle in Withered Wasteland

Water Gun is the first move you'll learn in Pokopia. It can be learned from Squirtle, one of the first Pokemon you'll encounter at Withered Wasteland.

How to Use Water Gun

Press ZR

Make sure you have Water Gun as your active move, which should be visible on the bottom right section of your screen. With Water Gun as your active move, five squares in a cross pattern should be highlighted in front of you, moving as you move.

Press ZR on your controller in order to water everything within the highlighted cross area.
