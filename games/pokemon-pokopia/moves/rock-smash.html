Rock Smash Guide

Learn how to use the Rock Smash move in Pokemon Pokopia

Rock Smash is a move that allows you to break rocks in Pokemon Pokopia. Check out the effects of Rock Smash, how to learn it, and how to use it.

Rock Smash Effects

Break Rocks

Rock Smash allows you to break rocks and boulders blocking your path. This is essential for exploring new areas and accessing hidden locations.

Obtain Materials

When you break rocks, you can obtain various materials including Stones and Ore. These materials are useful for crafting and building.

How to Learn Rock Smash

Find a Pokemon with Rock Smash

Rock Smash can be learned from various Pokemon throughout the game. Look for Fighting or Rock-type Pokemon that can teach you this move.

How to Use Rock Smash

Press ZR to Use Rock Smash

Make sure you have Rock Smash as your active move. Press ZR to break the rock in front of you.
