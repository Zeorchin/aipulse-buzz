Surf Guide

Learn how to use the Surf transformation move in Pokemon Pokopia

Home › Moves › Surf

Surf is a transformation move that allows you to ride on water in Pokemon Pokopia. Check out the effects of Surf, how to learn it, and how to use it.

Surf Effects

Travel on Water

Surf allows you to transform into a Pokemon that can travel across water. This opens up new areas and allows you to reach islands and locations that were previously inaccessible.

Access New Areas

Many areas in Pokopia are surrounded by water. Surf is essential for reaching places like Bleak Beach and other water-locked regions.

How to Learn Surf

Find a Water Pokemon

Surf can be learned from Water-type Pokemon. You can find them near bodies of water throughout the game, particularly in areas like Bleak Beach.

How to Use Surf

Press ZR to Transform

Make sure you have Surf as your active move. Press ZR near water to transform and start surfing across the water.

Use the control stick to navigate while surfing.
