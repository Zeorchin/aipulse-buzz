Waterfall Guide

Learn how to use the Waterfall transformation move in Pokemon Pokopia

Home › Moves › Waterfall

Waterfall is a transformation move that allows you to climb waterfalls in Pokemon Pokopia. Check out the effects of Waterfall, how to learn it, and how to use it.

Waterfall Effects

Climb Waterfalls

Waterfall allows you to climb up waterfalls, reaching areas that would otherwise be inaccessible. This is essential for exploring vertical areas and finding hidden items.

Access Hidden Areas

Many secret locations and rare Pokemon can be found at the top of waterfalls.

How to Learn Waterfall

Find a Water Pokemon

Waterfall can be learned from Water-type Pokemon that can climb. Look for Pokemon like Lapras, Vaporeon, or other strong swimming Pokemon.

How to Use Waterfall

Press ZR to Climb

Make sure you have Waterfall as your active move. Press ZR at the base of a waterfall to climb up.
