Camouflage Guide

Learn how to use the Camouflage move in Pokemon Pokopia

Camouflage is a move that allows you to blend into your surroundings in Pokemon Pokopia. Check out the effects of Camouflage, how to learn it, and how to use it.

Camouflage Effects

Blend Into Surroundings

Camouflage allows you to become invisible by blending into the environment. This can help you sneak up on shy Pokemon or avoid detection.

Special Interactions

Some areas and puzzles require Camouflage to complete. Look for disguised paths and hidden passages.

How to Learn Camouflage

Find a Pokemon with Camouflage

Camouflage can be learned from various Pokemon, particularly those known for blending in nature like Oddish, Bulbasaur, or Venonat.

How to Use Camouflage

Press ZR to Activate

Make sure you have Camouflage as your active move. Press ZR to become camouflaged in your current environment.
