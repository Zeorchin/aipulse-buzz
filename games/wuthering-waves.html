Overview

Wuthering Waves is an open-world action RPG developed by Kuro Games. Set in the post-apocalyptic world of Solaris-3, players take on the role of the Rover, exploring a vast landscape filled with mysteries, powerful enemies, and dynamic combat.

Key Features

  • Grapple & Glide: Seamless exploration with no stamina limits
  • Absorb Echoes: Transform into defeated enemies
  • Fast-Paced Combat: Dodge, parry, and chain combos
  • Open World: Explore the vast continent of Solaris-3
