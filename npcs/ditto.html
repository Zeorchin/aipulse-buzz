About Ditto

Ditto is a unique NPC in Pokemon Pokopia that can transform into various Pokemon houses. Players can recruit Ditto to live in their habitats as decorative housing.

Location

Ditto can be found wandering near the Pokemon houses in various areas. The main Ditto location is in Withered Wasteland near the abandoned houses.

How to Recruit

  • Complete the "Lost Ditto" request
  • Bring Ditto to your habitat
  • Ditto will transform into Pokemon houses
