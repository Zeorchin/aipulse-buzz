About Tinkmaster

Tinkmaster is the crafting and building expert in Pokemon Pokopia. He helps players learn advanced construction techniques and unlock new furniture recipes.

Location

Tinkmaster can be found at his workshop in Rocky Ridges. He operates a specialized crafting service for rare items.

Services

  • Crafting Recipes - Learn advanced furniture recipes
  • Building Tips - Get construction advice
  • Special Orders - Craft rare items
