How Long to Beat Pokemon Pokopia

Complete story and postgame playtime estimate

Home › Tips › How Long to Beat

Main Story

The main story of Pokemon Pokopia takes approximately 25-35 hours to complete. This includes:

  • Completing all 4 main areas (Withered Wasteland, Bleak Beach, Rocky Ridges, Sparkling Skylands)
  • Building and customizing your first few habitats
  • Recruiting around 50 Pokemon
  • Completing the main storyline quests

Main Story + Extras

Adding optional content extends playtime to 40-50 hours:

  • Completing all habitat customization
  • Recruiting all 150+ Pokemon
  • Finishing all NPC requests
  • Mastering all moves and crafting recipes

100% Completion

Achieving 100% completion takes approximately 80-100 hours:

  • All Pokemon recruited and evolved
  • All furniture and items collected
  • All achievements unlocked
  • All records and reels obtained
  • All specialties and artifacts collected
