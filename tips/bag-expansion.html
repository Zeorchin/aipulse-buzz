Bag Storage Tiers

Your bag has multiple storage categories, each of which can be expanded:

  • Items - General items and materials
  • Pokemon - Pokemon you're carrying
  • Furniture - Decorations for your island
  • Clothing - Outfits and accessories

How to Expand

Storage Type Default Max How to Expand
Items 30 100 Buy from Nook's Cranny
Pokemon 6 20 Complete requests
Furniture 100 500 Upgrade house storage
Clothing 30 80 Buy from Able Sisters

Tips

  • Prioritize expanding item storage early
  • Store excess furniture in your house
  • Sell duplicate items to make room
