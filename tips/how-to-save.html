How to Save Your Game

  1. Go to a Pokemon Center
  2. Select "Save Game" option
  3. Confirm your save
Note: The game auto-saves at certain points, but it's recommended to manually save regularly.

Save Points

  • Pokemon Centers
  • After completing requests
  • Before time traveling
  • When exiting the game
