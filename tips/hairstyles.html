Getting Hairstyles

Hairstyles can be obtained from the following sources:

  • Shampoodle - Main hair salon
  • Happy Home Paradise - Special styles
  • Special Events - Limited styles
  • amiibo - Exclusive styles

Hairstyle Categories

Special Hairstyles

Some hairstyles are only available through special methods:

  • Zipper - From Shampoodle after 50 haircuts
  • Modern - From Harriet
  • Retro - From Harriet
  • Balloon - From balloon messages
