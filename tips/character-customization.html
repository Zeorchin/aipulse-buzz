Character Customization

All appearance options and how to unlock them

Home › Tips › Character Customization

Hair Styles

There are over 50 different hair styles available. Some are unlocked by default while others require:

  • Reaching certain friendship levels
  • Completing specific requests
  • Purchasing from the Salon for 500 coins

Outfits

Customize your character's clothing with various outfits:

  • Casual Wear - Default outfits
  • Work Clothes - For crafting and building
  • Formal Wear - For special events
  • Seasonal Outfits - Limited time events

Accessories

  • Hats and caps
  • Glasses and sunglasses
  • Earrings and piercings
  • Backpacks and bags

How to Change Appearance

Visit the following locations to change your appearance:

  • Salon - Hair and basic customization
  • Clothing Store - Outfits and accessories
  • Photo Studio - Try different looks with filters
