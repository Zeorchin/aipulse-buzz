How to Build Habitats

Complete guide to building all habitat types

Home › Habitat › How to Build

Learn how to build habitats to attract Pokemon to your island in Pokemon Pokopia.

What Are Habitats?

Habitats are special areas where Pokemon live. Each Pokemon requires a specific type of habitat to be happy and stay on your island.

How to Build a Habitat

  1. Unlock the Ability - Build a Pokemon Center to unlock habitat building
  2. Gather Materials - Collect the required materials for your habitat
  3. Prepare the Land - Clear an area for your habitat
  4. Place Habitat Blocks - Build the required number of habitat blocks
  5. Create the Right Conditions - Adjust temperature, humidity, and lighting

Habitat Requirements

Each habitat type requires different conditions:

  • Grass Type: Tall grass patches
  • Water Type: Pond or water source
  • Fire Type: Near lava or heat source
  • Electric Type: Near power sources

Habitat Conditions

You can modify habitats to attract specific Pokemon:

  • Dark Habitat - For Ghost and Dark types
  • Bright Habitat - For Fire and Electric types
  • Dry Habitat - For Ground and Rock types
  • Humid Habitat - For Water and Grass types
