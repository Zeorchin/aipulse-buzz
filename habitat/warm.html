Warm Habitat Guide

Creating warm environments for Pokemon

Home › Habitat › Warm Habitat

Warm habitats are perfect for Fire-type and some Bug-type Pokemon.

How to Create a Warm Habitat

  1. Place fire-related decorations
  2. Use lava or heat sources
  3. Place campfires or torches
  4. Avoid ice and water nearby

Pokemon That Need Warm Habitats

  • Charmander
  • Charmeleon
  • Charizard
  • Vulpix
  • Magmar
  • Scyther

Habitat Conditions

  • Dark
  • Bright
  • Dry
  • Humid
  • Warm
  • Cool
