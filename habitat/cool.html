Cool Habitat Guide

How to make cool habitats for Ice-type Pokemon

Home › Habitat › Cool Habitat

Cool habitats are essential for Ice-type and some Water-type Pokemon.

How to Create a Cool Habitat

  1. Place ice-related decorations
  2. Use snow or ice blocks
  3. Avoid fire sources nearby
  4. Add snowflake decorations

Pokemon That Need Cool Habitats

  • Snowver
  • Abomasnow
  • Snover
  • Swinub
  • Piloswine

Habitat Conditions

  • Dark
  • Bright
  • Dry
  • Humid
  • Warm
  • Cool
